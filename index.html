Healthy eating goes beyond reaching a healthy weight


Healthy eating can also work as functional medicine, not just as a measure to control your weight. Put basically> if you want to be healthy, change your diet. Genes do not determine diseases on their own but function only by being activated, and nutrition plays a critical role in determining which genes are expressed. Equally, it has been proven that the nutrition of the mother, affects the foetus, and the nutrition of the foetus, goes on to effect the nutrition of their own offspring as foetuses. So there has never been a more crucial time to think about your diet.

The researcher
